Steps to make a Spicy Tuna Mayo Sandwich

  1. Take 2 slices of bread of choice or a bun (ie White Roll, Brioche Bun, etc).
  2. Toast (if you'd like it extra DELISH!), spread some butter on each side.
  3. Putting the bread to one side, in a small bowl, empty the contents of a drained tuna can into it.
    Add 2Tbsp of mayo, some salt and pepper (to taste), chilli flakes, squeeze of lime and some chopped spring onions, for a kick!
  4. Assemble your sandwich with half of this filling and top with some Rocket and ENJOY!
